Create an Indonesia map from Excel

Turn a table of regencies and cities into a presentation map without uploading your spreadsheet.

1. Add data

Paste data from Excel or Google Sheets, or upload a local CSV, TSV, or XLSX file.

2. Match regions

Review names with more than one match, names that were not found, and duplicates. The app never chooses an uncertain match silently.

3. Design and export

Color by category, equal ranges, ranked groups, manual ranges, or a midpoint. Export the result as SVG, PNG, PDF, or a match-report CSV.

Try a safe example

Start with the synthetic CSV sample, or follow the two-city example. The sample is not real statistical data. It exists only to show how the app works.

Who is this for?

This tool is for people who need a map for a presentation, internal report, or lightweight plan without using specialist mapping software. Your data stays in your browser. The app has no accounts, collaboration service, or cloud project storage.
