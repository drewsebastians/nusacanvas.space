Export a map to PowerPoint

Choose a file format and add the map details before saving it for a slide or report.

Choose a format

Use SVG when you want a scalable graphic for PowerPoint or a graphics editor. Use PNG for a ready-to-use image. A4 and A3 PDFs are available as local raster files for printing or sharing. Text in the PDF cannot be selected.

Choose the map area

All of Indonesia exports the national view. Current map view exports the area currently visible on the map. Choose the area explicitly so you do not export a small area by mistake.

Add map details

Enter a title, data source, and period. The app always adds the required boundary attribution. Check the legend title, footnote, and size before exporting.

An exported map is a visual reference, not a legal boundary record. For a very large image, your browser may ask for confirmation or use a lower resolution to finish the export.
