Terms of use

These are operational terms for the staging app. They are not professional legal advice.

Purpose

NusaCanvas helps you create colored region maps for visual reference. An exported map is not a legal boundary record, survey result, or sole official source.

Your responsibility

Check your data, source credits, context, and exported map before publishing it or using it for an important decision.

Third-party data and licenses

The app code uses the MIT License. Boundary data and third-party libraries remain subject to their source licenses. See Sources and licenses and ATTRIBUTION.md.

Service limits

This app does not provide accounts, cloud backups, official support, analytics, ads, or guaranteed availability.

Report a data issue

You can prepare a report with the data-report template. No public contact channel has been verified in the repository, so external delivery still requires an owner decision.
