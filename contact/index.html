Contact and data reports

No public email address or other contact channel has been verified in the repository. This page provides a report template that you can copy or download. It does not send anything automatically.

Contact status

The owner must choose a public contact destination before reports can be sent. Until then, do not add spreadsheet data, project contents, filenames, or personal information unless you intend to share it manually.

Report a data issue

Add only the information needed to explain the issue. The template includes the app, boundary, and region-list versions. It does not read your project file or spreadsheet.
